We are Draccon Tech

A multidisciplinary team participating in the Hackathon. We built SWARM — an autonomous AI ecosystem designed to explore exoplanets, secure communications, and synchronize knowledge with near-zero latency across deep space.

Our Mission

  • Transform stellar signals into actionable knowledge in near real time.
  • Bridge data science, engineering, and design for responsible exploration.
  • Reduce latency through autonomous edge computation and intelligent mediation.

What is SWARM

SWARM operates as a mesh of four artificial intelligences, working in harmony as a single collective mind:

AION

Exploration & Catalog — spectral scans, mapping, and planetary prioritization.

EXON

Security & Self-Heal — adaptive firewall, data integrity, and noise correction.

NOVA

Mediation & Routing — optimizes traffic, minimizes round-trip latency, and sends logs to base.

EIRYA

Core & Backbone — network server, model distributor, and global state synchronizer.

How It Works

  1. Detection (AION): Scans systems, identifies candidates, and builds dossiers.
  2. Protection (EXON): Validates packets, filters anomalies, and self-corrects errors.
  3. Routing (NOVA): Decides what stays at the edge, compresses data, and optimizes communication paths.
  4. Consensus (EIRYA): Consolidates knowledge, version-controls models, and replicates the swarm’s neural network.
